SERVICES

SERVICES: RAJARATA UNIVERSITY

Rajarata University has the services of a State University. Our University has been able to provide special facilities for students as well as lecturers. These services are provided for the academic and extracurricular activities of the university student.

LIBRARY

The Library, Rajarata University of Sri Lanka is pioneered for acquisition, organizing and distribution of knowledge and information to its users. It provides services to the university community such as Undergraduates, Postgraduates, Academic staff members, Administrative and non-academic staff members. In addition the library serves the knowledge and contribution to the researchers and outside community in the region.

SPORT & PHYSICAL EDUCATION

The Department of Physical Education (DOPE) of RUSL was established on 15th July 1997 and it provides a wide range of sports and recreational programmes for the weal of student community of the University.

CAREER GUIDANCE UNIT

The Career Guidance Unit of the University of Rajarata was created on 2005, through the initiative of the University Grants Commission (UGC), Sri Lanka. The unit functions directly under the Vice Chancellor. We are located on University main premises at Mihintale as each faculty has the quick access. It is a one story building with 100 people capacity. The unit is well functioned under the director and supported coordinators from each faculty.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Faculty of Agriculture , Rajarata University of Sri Lanka extends the boundaries of the faculty to the boundaries of the region through its extension and outreach arm to provide access to faculty resources and research so that people of Rajarata can learn, be benefited and grow to enhance and improve their lives, All the three departments of the faculty of Agriculture have extended their services towards the community via faculty Outreach Centre to strengthen the adjacent rural communities, to support the development of both the government and private sector regional institutions and other organizations to actively and dedicatedly contribute to the rapid development of the country in the processes of making Sri Lanka the miracle of Asia.

CULTURE CENTER

Preparation and implementation of programmes for preservation, propagation and expansion of Literary Arts and Cultural activities maintaining Sri Lankan Identity.

HOSTAL & ACCOMMODATION

Any Undergraduate who resides 40Km away from the university premises, is eligible for accommodation facilities. The University provides good quality and continuing accommodation facilities by its own large hostel complexes and rented Houses.
